Methodology

How The Identification Works

The platform evaluates storage behavior at the day level, rolls evidence up to the meter level, and then assigns an enterprise-level conclusion.

Three-stage logic

  1. Day-level detection checks whether a daily load profile shows repeated charge/discharge behavior.
  2. Meter-level scoring aggregates repeated evidence over the full observation period.
  3. Enterprise-level scoring uses the strongest supporting meters to classify each enterprise.
